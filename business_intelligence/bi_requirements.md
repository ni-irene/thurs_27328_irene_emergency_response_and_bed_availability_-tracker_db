# Business Intelligence Requirements

## Overview
The Emergency Response and Bed Availability Tracker supports decision-making through database-driven analytics.
This document outlines the business intelligence requirements, stakeholders, and reporting needs.

---

## Stakeholders
- Hospital Administrators
- Emergency Department Managers
- Ward Supervisors
- Compliance and Audit Officers
- IT Database Administrators

---

## Decision Support Needs
- Identify wards with high bed occupancy
- Monitor emergency patient admission trends
- Detect frequent policy violations
- Assess bed utilization efficiency
- Support resource allocation decisions

---

## Reporting Frequency
- Daily: Bed availability and emergency admissions
- Weekly: Audit violations and user activity
- Monthly: Occupancy trends and performance metrics

---

## Data Sources
- WARDS
- BEDS
- PATIENTS
- ADMISSIONS
- AUDIT_LOG
- PUBLIC_HOLIDAYS
