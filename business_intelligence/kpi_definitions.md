# Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Definitions

## 1. Bed Occupancy Rate
- Description: Percentage of occupied beds per ward
- Formula:
  (Occupied Beds / Total Beds) Ã— 100
- Purpose: Identifies overcrowded wards

---

## 2. Available Beds Per Ward
- Description: Number of available beds in each ward
- Purpose: Supports real-time admission decisions

---

## 3. Emergency Admission Rate
- Description: Total number of emergency admissions per day
- Purpose: Measures emergency workload

---

## 4. Average Length of Stay
- Description: Average number of days patients stay admitted
- Purpose: Evaluates patient flow efficiency

---

## 5. Audit Violation Count
- Description: Number of denied database operations
- Purpose: Measures compliance with operational policies
